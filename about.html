About Our Company

Our History

Founded in 2010 in Juba, Vision To Action Engineering started as a small block-making workshop. Over the years, we expanded into full-service engineering, combining local expertise with global standards. Today, we are trusted by developers and homeowners alike for our integrity and quality.

Our team includes certified engineers, skilled craftsmen, and project managers who work collaboratively to deliver exceptional results.

Our Mission

To provide innovative engineering solutions that enhance communities, ensuring safety, sustainability, and aesthetic excellence.

Our Vision

To be the most respected engineering and construction company in South Sudan, known for quality, integrity, and client satisfaction.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We do what we promise.
  • Quality: No compromise on materials or workmanship.
  • Innovation: Embracing modern techniques.
  • Community: Building a better South Sudan.
